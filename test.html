nvid

for people who still measure web pages in kilobytes

Youtube embeds are big, even before you click to start watching one.

It would be better not to load all that stuff until you've clicked to watch.

Just give this script some things to click on. Clicking transforms them into a auto-playing videos. The experience is identical to a straight Youtube embed, but it's an order of magnitude lighter.
